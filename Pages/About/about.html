The Miranda Story

Miranda the Label is an established clothing brand based in Athens, Greece. Our Collections are produced in Greece and designed in Italy where the Creative director, Inna Lamprou, lives.


We are a female-founded brand by Inna Lamprou, who wanted to build a brand that sits in between high fashion and fast fashion, with great attention to detail and quality but yet affordable. Day to night pieces that are easy to wear and will live in the closet forever. Our Goal is to make women feel comfortable into their own skin yet effortlessly sexy, confident and empowered at the same time.

Miranda, is not just a label is a feeling. Every single piece tells a story. We design for ourselves, the modern woman, combining fabrics and patterns, old and new in order to mix & match designs between them and creat total outfits for each occasion. Our philoshophy is to design pieces that last in time and so our consumers buy well, choose less, and make it last!

Miranda is a fashion world of effortlessly cool outfits, stand out looks that make you feel confident, sexy and strong. Ready to wear clothes. High street fashion offering an effortless range of styles for all occasions. Each collection will have a print or shape for casual daywear to cocktail, evening looks or stand out beach style.

Design Process

Dressing is such an important part of our lives and what we do every day.

We design clothes for women. (Miranda Girl) that want to feel always at their best. What you really want to wear?

This is the starting point for designing every single piece of all our collections.

The design process starts with a cup of coffee and our fav music while imagining our dream cloths.

So we bust out our creativity, pencils, scissors and get started. We put all our love into the very fiber and fabrics of our designs.

Once complete, every piece is sent directly to our cyber world so as one day it can find a new home.
